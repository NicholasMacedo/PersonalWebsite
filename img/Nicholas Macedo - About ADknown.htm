About ADknown

A cutting-edge online media company

ADknown is a tech company that is located in the core of downtown Guelph within Old Quebec Street mall. As a tech company, ADknown develops in-house websites and applications to leverage targeted traffic from online platforms. This traffic would then be directed to one of ADknown’s content specific landing pages which contain advertisements.

ADknown’s employees are split up into three main groups. Developers, Search Engine Marketers (SEM), and Game Developers. The developers (DEV) team works on the various websites, internal tools and cost/revenue monitoring system for ADknown. The Search Engine Marketers (SEM) team creates ad campaigns and runs traffic to ADknown’s websites along with many other revenue generating tasks. The Game Developers team creates games for the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Being an employee of ADknown has many perks including: free drinks (including Monster energy drink), pool and foosball table, free weekly team lunches, and most importantly free coffee! Weekly team lunches allow for each team member to get to know each other better on a friendlier level than typical co-workers. This allows for easier interactions and higher cohesion between employees.
